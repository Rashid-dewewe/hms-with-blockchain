Key Features of the System
User Roles:

Doctor: Can view and manage patient records and appointments.

Patient: Can view their own records and manage appointments.

Blockchain Integration:

Use blockchain to securely store and manage health records and transactions.

Each user (doctor or patient) will have a unique blockchain address or identity.

Authentication:

Users register with their email, password, and role (Doctor or Patient).

During login, the system validates their credentials and assigns the appropriate role.

Role-Based Access Control:

Doctors and patients have access to different features based on their roles.
